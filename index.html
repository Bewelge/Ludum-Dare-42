By Bewelge
In this game, You have to try and launch Satellites into Space!

Choose the right settings to find a stable orbit and avoid them crashing into each other!



The Settings:
General

- "Launch Frequency":
How quickly the next Rocket will launch (Higher Values -> Quicker Spawn).

- "Update Delay":
Update Interval in Miliseconds (Lower Values -> Quicker Game).

- "Update Delay":
Update Interval in Miliseconds (Lower Values -> Quicker Game).


Rocket

- "Tilt":
The amount the Rocket will turn sideways during the specific stage.

- "Force":
The amount of powerthe specific stage of the rocket will use (Higher values reduce Fuel quicker!).

- "Fuel":
How much fuel to pack onto the Rocket stage. Less fuel means that part of the rocket becomes lighter. When fuel goes empty, the stage will be dropped.


Satellites

- "Thrust Interval":
The amount of ticks between thrusts (Lower Values -> Quicker Intervals)

- "Thrust Duration":
The amount of ticks that thrust is used every interval.

- "Thrust Force":
The amount of force that will be used when thrust is used each tick.
